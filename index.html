About Us

Welcome to Globe India Trip, your gateway to the breathtaking beauty and culture of the Kashmir Valley and Laddakh. Established in 2016 by our passionate founder, Mr. Mustakim, our travel company has been dedicated to curating unforgettable experiences in one of the most enchanting destinations on Earth.

Our Mission

At Globe India Trip, our mission is to be your trusted partner in exploring the diverse and enchanting tapestry of India.Our goal is to create lasting memories for our clients and contribute positively to the communities and environments we touch.

Our Vision

Our vision at Globe India Trip, is to become the premier travel company for discovering India's wonders. We aspire to inspire wanderlust in travelers from around the world by offering meticulously crafted itineraries, exceptional customer service, and immersive cultural experiences.
